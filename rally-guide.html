Critical: First Slot Hero Sets Rally Bonuses!

The hero in your FIRST SLOT determines the bonuses for the entire rally. This is the most important decision for rally participation!

Rally Leader Strategy

Your Role:

As rally leader, you initiate the attack and coordinate the assault. Your troops form the core of the rally.

Hero Selection:

Choose your STRONGEST hero for first slot, regardless of type:

  • Highest level hero
  • Best gear equipped
  • Maximum skill upgrades
  • Hero type doesn't matter - power does!

Pro Tip: Your strongest hero maximizes the rally capacity and overall damage output.

Rally Joiner Strategy

Your Role:

As a rally joiner, you reinforce the leader's attack. Your first slot hero determines your contribution type.

Hero Selection by Rally Type:

Attack Rally: Use Jessie in first slot (JA6 or JA5)
Defense Rally: Use Patrick in first slot (PD6 or PD5)

Pro Tip: Match your hero type to the rally objective for maximum effectiveness!

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Mistake #1

Using wrong hero type as rally joiner

This reduces your effectiveness and wastes troops

Mistake #2

Not checking first slot before joining rally

Always verify your squad's first hero matches the rally type

Mistake #3

Rally leader not using strongest hero

This limits rally capacity and reduces overall damage

Mistake #4

Using same squad for all rally types

Have dedicated attack and defense squads ready

Remember These Key Points

Rally Leaders:

  • • Always use your STRONGEST hero
  • • Hero type doesn't matter - power does
  • • Higher power = larger rally capacity

Rally Joiners:

  • • Attack rally = Jessie first slot
  • • Defense rally = Patrick first slot
  • • Match hero to rally objective

First slot hero selection can make or break your rally success!
